Kalam was a renowned scientist also known as theMissile Man of India. Read here to know more about his life and achievements. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was elected as India's 11th president from 2002-2007 and died in 2015 while he was delivering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong at the age of 84. Kalam was elected as the 11th president of India in 2002 with the support of both the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party and the then-opposition Indian National Congress. Widely referred to as the "People's President",[6] he returned to his civilian life of education, writing and public service after a single term.
